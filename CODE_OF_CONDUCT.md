# Atlas Platform Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Atlas Platform is committed to providing a professional, respectful and collaborative environment for all contributors.

We believe the best engineering outcomes are achieved through technical excellence, constructive feedback and mutual respect.

---

## Expected Behavior

Contributors are expected to:

- Be respectful
- Be professional
- Be constructive
- Share knowledge openly
- Focus on technical discussion
- Welcome diverse perspectives

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

- Harassment
- Personal attacks
- Discrimination
- Toxic communication
- Public shaming
- Intimidation

---

## Engineering Culture

Atlas Platform promotes:

- Open collaboration
- Technical excellence
- Continuous learning
- Professional conduct
- Community growth

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers reserve the right to remove content, comments or contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.
